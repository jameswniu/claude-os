# Team Processes

As part of an exercise in October 2021, we asked each team lead to summarize their team's processes. Here were the goals of the exercise:

  * **Onboarding** \- When new team members come on board, what are expectations for them and the team?
  * **Sharing** \- Teams have autonomy to do things how they want, but maybe other ways could be better? Having an understanding how teams do things differently can provide ideas to suggest changes yourself.
  * **Retros** \- it can useful to point back to process and ask whether there are issues in the process or if the agreed upon process isn't being followed.
  * **Values** \- A team's process defines what they value. Provides a place of reflection to ask whether what we've been doing is an accurate reflection of the values we want our team to follow.
  * **Alignment** \- Does the team agree on their processes? Does everyone understand expectations of themselves and their teammates?
