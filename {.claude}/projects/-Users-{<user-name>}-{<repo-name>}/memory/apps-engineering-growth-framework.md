## Intro

The goal of the Engineering Growth Framework is to provide an open-ended dialog between managers and reports to facilitate growth and improvement. The intent of the framework is not to act as a rubric, grading system, or competitive tool, but to provide a starting point for an individual's growth and alignment with the goals and [core values]((internal URL)) of our team. It's also important to note that no individual will fit into a clean set of boxes. As an example, an individual might exhibit Level 3 behavior for one task, but Level 1 for another task. These lists here are not checkboxes to complete, but rather help outline opportunities for growth.

This document is meant to evolve, and our goal is to allow flexibility for revisions and other enhancements that reveal themselves throughout ongoing conversations.

## Culture

---|---|---|---|---|---  
Level 1  
"Apprentice"| 

  * Competent in at least one language/framework in our stack
  * Able to contribute and fix issues on existing components
  * Able to write unit tests with best practices / proper coverage
  * Participates in design discussions
  * Follows coding guidelines
  * May require guidance for more complex tasks
  * Eager to learn as much as possible

| 

  * Works to build a strong relationship with teammates and manager; requests help when needed
  * Able to talk through problems non-judgmentally and with ego in check
  * Recognizes potential gaps in knowledge and works with Product/QA/Design to ensure tasks are fully understood
  * Seeks out feedback to use as a tool for growth
  * Remains respectful at all times, and works with teammates to resolve disagreements in a healthy manner

| 

  * Sets an example with solid work ethic and desire to learn
  * Able to take feedback with an open mind and show improvement with a positive attitude
  * Helps new hires get setup
  * Takes full responsibility to tasks assigned
  * Seeks out learning opportunities

| 

  * Understands the basic functionality of the team's core product
  * Able to understand and execute on business requirements
  * Understands team's product domain and how it relates to Centro's business goals
  * Understands who product's users are and why they use our application(s)

| 

  * Understands Centro's principles and values
  * Consistently brings a positive attitude to work

  
Level 2  
"Professional"| 

  * Solid understanding of full tech stack or deep understanding of a specialized area
  * Uses a systematic approach to investigate and resolve production issues
  * Understands how various components interact
  * Able to build new features from scratch following product requirements
  * Prioritizes quality by maintaining low production bug counts
  * Actively debugs production issues
  * Champions Software Engineering best practices
  * Consistently writes maintainable and performant code

| 

  * Committed to knowledge transfer and shared learning
  * Effectively communicates both technically and non-technically
  * Works with teammates to solve complex issues
  * Able to quickly identify and communicate potential blockers
  * Prioritizes design/product review
  * Approaches disagreement with inquisitiveness and an open mind

| 

  * Excellent team contributor and able to help others improve through code/functional/peer review
  * Sets the example for how to be accountable
  * Able and willing to help teammates improve
  * Provides constructive feedback for teammates
  * Delivers on commitments
  * Sees features and tasks all the way through to supporting in Production
  * Thinks strategically about technical direction

| 

  * Understands the organization's engineering strategy and how it fits into the overall business
  * Solid understanding of the majority of the team's core product
  * Able to comprehend and reiterate business logic to team members
  * Able to take business concepts and convert them into technical models
  * Adept at gathering product feedback and understanding users' point of view
  * Understands the role of Centro and its products play in the market
  * Understands the business impact of feature requirements

| 

  * Embodies Centro's [core principles](https://www.centro.net/company/culture/)
  * Goes above & beyond for teammates
  * Embraces diversity of thought across the team
  * Brings open-mindedness, inclusion and a sense of community
  * Helps with recruiting
  * Shows commitment to learning and growing

  
Level 3  
"Expert"| 

  * Deep understanding of more than one part of the stack
  * Able to design complex features that affect multiple components
  * Solid understanding how all the pieces within a project fit together
  * Expected to be an important voice in deciding technical direction and design
  * Able to work on complex tasks and get to the root of problems without guidance
  * Understands and contributes to testing strategy
  * Helps define best practices

| 

  * Frequently helps others resolve blockers and overcome obstacles
  * Able to lend expertise in a group setting while remaining open to opinions/suggestions of others
  * Works closely with Product/QA to identify dependencies and UX issues
  * Encourages teammates to openly share opinions

| 

  * Able to lead technical projects or features involving multiple engineers
  * Takes ownership in features delivered and understands that "development" is only a small part of seeing something through to release/support
  * Provides mentorship and guidance to teammates
  * Views code reviews as a teaching opportunity for best practices
  * Takes initiative to improve the team and processes
  * Actively explores ways to improve our products
  * Acknowledges weaknesses and works with others to fill individual gaps

| 

  * Able to translate user stories into potential solutions that fill customers' needs
  * Able to anticipate potential problems based on feature requirements
  * Broad understanding of overall product direction and purpose
  * Complete understanding of the team's product and can explain how data models map to user-facing requirements
  * Has a thorough understanding of multiple of Centro's lines of business
  * Understands how Centro integrates with its partners (e.g. Google, Facebook, etc) and how each of those products fit into the overall market
  * Understands how technical decisions affect the user experience
  * Able to explain the business impact of requirements to team members

| 

  * Provides Raving Fan service by going above & beyond for fellow Engineering Team members including Product/QA/Design/Prod Ops
  * Works to increase diversity in recruitment
  * Participates in Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) events and discussions
  * Represents Centro in the community by attending external events/meetups
  * Seeks out learning opportunities beyond sprint work

  
Level 4  
"Guru"| 

  * Expert knowledge across the tech stack as well as how components interact
  * Able to solve the hardest problems; a "go-to" source for all things technical
  * Has intimate knowledge of overall architecture and how all the components interact
  * Able to architect complete solutions that affect multiple products/teams
  * Helps drive overall system testing strategy
  * Establishes best practices and writes code which embodies them

| 

  * Consistently works across teams to help others resolve blockers and fill any gaps in understanding
  * Ensures that credit is shared and given where due
  * Takes ownership of decisions made across teams, ensuring they align with organizational goals and understands the impact within those teams
  * Works with other teams to ensure cross-platform dependencies are met
  * Focused on leading respectful, rational discussions and acts as a mediator for others
  * Channels negativity into inspiration and motivation

| 

  * Expert at coaching and mentoring others
  * Sets and aligns future strategy and vision
  * Trusted and able to lead large projects that may span multiple teams
  * Inspires teammates to be best they can be
  * Sets a high bar for delivery and inspires others to be accountable
  * Relentless at improving code and refining processes
  * Guides team in architecting optimal technical solutions

| 

  * The go-to person for business questions
  * Appreciates how new features will affect existing components
  * Has a long-term vision for the product and gives guidance on considering the wider impact
  * Seeks out user issues and suggests solutions to Engineering/Product/Design
  * Able to fully comprehend the business impact of tasks
  * Challenges requirements which may not have the desired impact
  * Able to take large business requirements and design complete end-to-end technical solutions
  * Has a complete understanding of Centro's lines of business
  * Can explain the primary players of the media industry and how they interact with each other

| 

  * Provides Raving Fan service to other internal organizations and external users/customers/partners
  * Considers and discusses ways to improve equity within the team
  * Considers and discusses ways to improve inclusion within the team
  * Builds and leads DEI initiatives
  * Actively promotes Centro in the community by engaging others at external events/meetups
